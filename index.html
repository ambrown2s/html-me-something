After several months immersed in Asian culture, I came back to the states. Where I landed in the brisk north. The state of a thousand lakes.

After some time in the bitter cold. I went to the nations capitol, where i fell in loved with city nicknamed "chocalate city". The art, muesuems and culture captured my heart very much.
